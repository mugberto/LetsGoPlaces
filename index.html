LET'S GO PLACES WORKS IN FOUR EASY STEPS

1-SEARCH

It's simple. Type in the search field a keyword related to your favorite destination. Such as the type of terrain(mountain, lac, ...), location name, distance or a combination of keywords. Let's Go Places will come up with interesting suggestions from which you can choose your destination.

2-LOCATE

Choose one destination in the search resulting list of suggestions to learn about it. You can always learn about other suggestions to find the most attractive destination.

3-MAKE BUDGET

Let's Go Places gives information on the cost of all your trip needs. Such as transport, restaurant, logde and other types of leisure in the chosen destination. It Will estimate the total cost with respect to how you want your trip to be like.

4-RELAX

Once you have all the basic information on your destination of choice. You can now relax and read our blog to learn more about the region culture, history, cuisine, etc.
